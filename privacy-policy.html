Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

V2V Technologies Inc. ("we", "our", "us") operates cloud-based business management applications including GEM-BOOKS and GEM-CAR. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information, including data accessed through Google services.

Information We Collect

When you use our applications, we may collect the following information:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and business information provided during account registration
  • Usage data and activity logs to improve our services
  • Google authentication tokens when you connect your Google/Gmail account

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our business management services
  • Send business emails (invoices, quotes, notifications) on your behalf through your connected Gmail account
  • Provide customer support and respond to your requests
  • Improve and optimize our applications

Google User Data

Our applications use Google OAuth 2.0 to connect to your Gmail account. We request access to the following scope:

  • gmail.send — to send emails (invoices, quotes, business communications) on your behalf when you initiate a send action within our application

Regarding your Google data:

  • We only send emails that you explicitly initiate within our application
  • We do not read, scan, or access the contents of your mailbox
  • We store only the authentication tokens necessary to send emails on your behalf
  • We do not share, sell, or transfer your Google data to any third parties
  • We do not use your Google data for advertising, analytics, or AI/ML training purposes
  • You can revoke access at any time through your Google Account Permissions

Our use of Google user data complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except when required by law or to protect our legal rights.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including TLS encryption to protect your data in transit and at rest. Access to user data is restricted to authorized employees bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. Authentication tokens are stored securely and can be revoked by you at any time. When you disconnect your Google account or delete your account, associated tokens are removed.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may revoke Google access at any time. To exercise your rights, contact us at the address below.

Compliance

This policy complies with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for EU residents, and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) for California residents.
